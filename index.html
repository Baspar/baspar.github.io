Hey there!

I'm Bastien, a 26 y.o. fullstack developer living, working and wandering in Singapore.
I graduated with a MSc in Applied Mathematics from a French college in 2016, and have been tikering around since then.


Among the things that define me:

  • I'm a Linux user since 10 years, starting with Ubuntu Hardy Heron, Slackware and I finally set my sights on Archlinux 5 years ago.
  • I'm a stubborn Vim user for more than 5 years, and I'm wasting investing way too much time tweaking my config files.
  • I'm a boardgamer, always ready for a good game of 7th Continent or Agricola
  • I got dragged into Clojure by a old beardy wizard a few years back, and I'm now playing with Rust and algorithms
  • I'm all set and ready for Advent of Code every year, just to challenge myself and dip into new languages.

Feel free to take a look at my blog and miniblog to see what am I up to.
If you're looking for me online, you can always find me on Github, or contact me on Telegram or via mail

See you around!
